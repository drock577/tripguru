Atlantis, Paradise Island Bahamas

Aquaventure (You’ll want to spend at least a couple days here to do everything the park has to offer)

Day: Whether you are traveling alone, with your friends, or with your family, Atlantis in the Bahamas is the place to start! Atlantis on paradise island has everything you could ever want from the world’s largest open-air marine habitat; to Aquaventure, a 141-acre water park, famous for the iconic Mayan Temple’s Leap of Faith waterslide. The park has 11 stunning pools to kick back and relax and have a drink.

The Ocean Club Golf Course

$240-295 /round

Morning or Day: Paradise Island has over 5 miles of magnificent beaches with a spectacular waterfront golf course designed by Tom Weiskopf. The Ocean Club Golf Course is truly a golfer’s paradise and offers its players every detail for a perfect round in the Caribbean. The eighteen-hole, par 72 course challenges even the most experienced players with crosswinds and holes next to the water!

A relaxing day at the Mandara Spa

Morning or Night: If you're looking for another way to relax, go just past The Royal Hotel to the award-winning Mandara Spa. The spa brings together the ancient Balinese healing touches, traditional European therapies, and natural elements to The Bahamas. The spa is open from 8am to 9pm, making it a perfect thing to do first thing in the morning, or after a long day in the sun. Couples can enjoy the Couples Spa Suite for a relaxing aromatherapy. Before booking your day at the spa, be sure to visit the website and locate the promo code for that month and save some money!

The Atlantis Casino, Atlantis

Night: Experience the thrill of gaming at The Caribbean’s premier Casino, The Atlantis Casino. The casino is a masterpiece in itself with incredible glass sculptures, filled with colorful and vibrant energy. At the casino, you can play on over 700 different slot machines or play at any table of the 85 gaming tables. These gaming tables are at your disposal with games including blackjack, Texas Hold’em poker, roulette, and high roller. If you are new to the casino life, don’t worry; complimentary daily gaming lessons are available every day.

Best places to eat:

The hotel and meal plan packages are the best way to go beyond a doubt! These packages are the easiest way to save money AND still eat AMAZING food at many 5 star restaurants! The food packages vary based on how many meals you want to eat a day. You can select any combination of breakfast, lunch, and dinner, or all three!

  • Poseiden’s Table offers an over-the-top breakfast buffet.
  • Frankie Gone Bananas is a true bahamian restaurant and has been a favorite of Nassau residents for years.
  • Enjoy lunch and brunch on the beach at Sip Sip where celebrity chef Julie Lightbourn brings her magnificent harbour menu on the sand.
  • Looking for some fine dining? At the Cove, celebrity and master chef Jose Andres unveiled his stunning new restaurant, Fish, featuring the freshest Bahamian seafood.

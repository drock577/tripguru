3 Best Instagram Worthy Spots in Breckenridge

Posted by Trip Guru on Febuary 1, 2019

Location 1: If you’re looking for a site to see for miles look no further than Mohawk Trail. It’s about 20 minutes from Breckenridge and boasts views over 2,000 feet higher in the air above the forest. It’s a treacherous 8.6 mile hike round trip but will prove to be worth it and you will get one of the most breath taking views.

Along the way up there will be rushing waterfalls that you can get right up next to and feel the fresh water. You may even see some animals along the way including the amazing yet friendly mountain goat. At the top you can expect to see a few different natural lakes and maybe even snow depending on the time of year.

Location 2: Next we have the heart of Breckenridge in their main street downtown which has a significant history that can’t be replicated. Main street goes for a few miles filled with a large variety of colorful buildings dating back to the early 1900’s. If your desire is to capture the town heritage in pictures this the place for you because there will be anything from local snowboard shops to restaurants that have been in families for years. One of my favorite places to get an awesome picture is Breck Sports with their 8 foot bear sculpture you can get an Instagram worthy photo.

Location 3: To get a better picture of the town you can take a short drive up Ski Hill Road which takes you a little further into nature up the mountain. There is a small parking lot where you can capture a picture of the whole city in all its glory. Although I do recommend going up at night because Breckenridge is always lit up with lights at night which make for a very bright and joyful picture. You can see the whole town and can even get the stars from up above in the picture if angled correctly!

Los Angeles

California

Method of transport: Car or rental car. Public transport is not advisable in Los Angeles.

  • HIGHLIGHTS:
  • Day 1: Art, Beaches, People Watching, and shopping
  • Day 2: Hollywood - Shopping, museums, & hiking
  • Day 3: Universal Studios & Universal Citywalk
  • RECOMMENDED STAY:
  • Budget: HI Los Angeles - Santa Monica - $32 / night
  • Comfortable: Foghorn Harbor Inn
  • Luxury: Loews Santa Monica

Breckenridge

Colorado

Method of transport: Personal car, Rental car, or Walking.

Note: Days vary depending on the season for your trip.

  • HIGHLIGHTS:
  • Day 1: Downtown Breckenridge and Shopping
  • Day 2 (Summer): Hiking and White Water Rafting
  • Day 2 (Winter): Snowboarding/Skiing
  • Day 3: Haunted Tour, Gondola Ride, and Animal Spotting
  • RECOMMENDED STAY:
  • Budget: The Bivvi - $89 / night
  • Comfortable: Condo Rental (best for a large group) $100-200 / night
  • Luxury: Crystal Peak Lodge - $606 / night
